Banana Muffin

Description

This delicious banana muffin can be made using just a few key ingredients.

Delicious and nutritious, just follow this recipe for a batch of tasty banana muffins.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Bananas
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 12-cup muffin tin. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Mix bananas, sugar, egg, and melted butter in a separate large bowl until well combined; fold in flour mixture until smooth. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 2/3 full.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, about 25 to 30 minutes. Cool briefly in the tin, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
